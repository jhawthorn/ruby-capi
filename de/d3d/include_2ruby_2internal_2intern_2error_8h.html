Ensures that the passed integer is in the passed range.

When you can use rb_scan_args() that is preferred over this one (powerful, descriptive). But it can have its own application area.

Parameters
[in]argcArbitrary integer.
[in]minMinimum allowed argv.
[in]maxMaximum allowed argv, or UNLIMITED_ARGUMENTS.
Exceptions
rb_eArgError`argc` out of range.
Returns
The passed argc.
Postcondition
Upon successful return argc is in range of min..max, both inclusive.
